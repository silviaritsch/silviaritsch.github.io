Silvia Ritsch is a doctoral student in cryptography in the Applied Cryptography and Provable Security Group @ Eindhoven University of Technology under supervision of [Andreas Huelsing](https://huelsing.net/) and [Kathrin Hoevelmanns](https://hoevelmanns.net/).
Her research interests include password-authenticated key exchange and its security proofs, in particular against attackers with quantum computing capabilities.

She's a member of the Quantum Safe Internet (QSI) doctoral network, working on securing key exchange under quantum attack.

Silvia studied Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at ETH Zurich, with a focus on Network Security and Applied Cryptography.
